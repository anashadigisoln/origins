About Us

Who We Are

Origin's Spices: Your Trusted Global Spice Partner Origin's Spices is a Natural Brand of Shrawani Farmfood India LLP which is a SHRWANI GROUP of company building expertise in international spice sourcing and supply chain management.

We are dedicated to providing global buyers with premium Indian spices, ensuring both purity and reliability in every shipment. Our business is founded on ethical partnerships: we work directly with small farmers and cooperatives across India. This collaborative approach allows us to maintain exceptional product quality, guarantee complete traceability, and ensure fair pricing and sustainable practices at the source.
